    Dragon Tiger

    The perfect game for quick Players.

    Blockchain Dragon Tiger is a popular high-end live game in Asian region. In this table game, winners are determined by the size of cards instead of their suits. King is the largest, while Ace is the smallest.

    FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

    Simple,
    easy to play

    Dragon Tiger is a two-card game betting on which hand will be higher. This fast-paced and straightforward game has only three outcomes: "Dragon", "Tiger", and "Tie".

    Legible
    Scoreboards

    In the Game Lobby, players only need to select the Professional scoreboard, and they can see the winning statistics of Dragon Tiger of current shoes and last 100 rounds; After entering the Live Game, they can see more scoreboard, including “Bead Road”, “Big Road”, “Big Eye Boy”, “Small Road” and the “Cockroach Pig”.

    Predict the next outcome by
    “Next Banker” or “ Next Player”

    WE understand that many Dragon Tiger lovers like to track the past scores and results to predict the next outcome, so our "Next Banker" and “Next Player” function allows players to make predictions and place bets easily.
